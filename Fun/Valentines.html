What you mean to me

You brought me sunshine

when I only saw rain,

You brought me laughter

when i only felt pain

I will Love you

as long as the sun

burns in the sky,

as long as the moon

shines its light

into the dark night,

until the raging

blue oceans become

calm and run dry.

I will love you untill the end of time.
